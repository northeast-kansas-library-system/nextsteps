*******************************
Koha Open Source Library System
*******************************

Overview
========

Koha is a fully featured, scalable :term:`library management system.<Integrated library system>` Development is sponsored by libraries of varying types and sizes, volunteers, and support companies worldwide.

Wikipedia has a nice article outlining the history and origins of Koha at `<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Koha_(software)>`_

Next Search Catalog has been using Koha as its :term:`ILS<Integrated library system>` since 2008 (see :ref:`About us` for more information).


Website and community
=====================

You can find the Koha community website at `https://koha-community.org <https://koha-community.org>`_

koha-US
=======

koha-US is a 501(3)(c) non-profit dedicated to helping Koha users in the United States of America.  NEKLS has had a long history of of working with and supporting koha-US.
