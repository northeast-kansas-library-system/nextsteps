How to log in to the Koha staff interface

Logging in

  1. Go to https://staff.nekls.bywatersolutions.com

  2. Enter your username

  3. Enter your password

  4. Click the button that says “Log in”

  5. If you successfully log in, you’ll see the Next Search Catalog home page for staff

  6. If you are unsuccessful, go to Problems you may encounter
