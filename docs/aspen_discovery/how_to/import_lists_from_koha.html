How to import lists from Koha

If a borrower had created lists in the Koha OPAC and they want to transfer those lists into Aspen Discovery, follow these steps:

  1. Log into Aspen Discovery and click on “Your Lists”

  1. Click on the “Import From Old Catalog” button

  1. Aspen Discovery will automatically re-create the Koha OPAC list in Aspen Discovery

  1. Go to “Your Lists” to see the imported list

  1. When you open the list in Aspen Discovery it will have the same contents as the list in the Koha OPAC
