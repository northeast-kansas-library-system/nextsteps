Log in to the staff interface

  1. Open staff.nextkansas.org

  1. Enter your username and password where indicated and click on “Log in”

  1. If you’re successful you’ll be taken to the home page in the staff interface

  1. If you enter an incorrect login/password combination 5 or more times in a row you will see a “You must reset your password” message on the login page

  • If you are locked out, there will be a link to a video tutorial on resetting your password at the top of the page
