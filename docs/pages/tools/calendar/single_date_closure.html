Calendar - close on a specific date

  1. On the home page click on “Tools”

  2. In the right hand column, click on “Calendar”

  3. To add a closure on a single date, use the calendar to find the date and then click on the date of the closure

  4. Give the closure a title

  5. Add a description if you desire

  6. Make sure the radio button says “Holiday only on this day”

  7. Click on “Save”

  8. Confirm that the closure is now in the list on the right
