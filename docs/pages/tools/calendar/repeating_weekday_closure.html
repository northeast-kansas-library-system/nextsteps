Calendar - close on the same day every week

  1. On the home page click on “Tools”

  2. In the right hand column, click on “Calendar”

  3. To add a closure on every Saturday, click on any Saturday on the calendar

  4. Give the closure a title

  5. Add a description if you desire

  6. Make sure the radio button says “Holiday repeated every same day of the week”

  7. Click on “Save”

  8. Confirm that the closures are now in the list on the right
