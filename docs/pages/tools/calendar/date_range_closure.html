Calendar - close a range of dates

  1. On the home page click on “Tools”

  2. In the right hand column, click on “Calendar”

  3. To add a closure on a range of dates, use the calendar to find the starting date for the closure and then click on it

  4. Give the closure a title

  5. Add a description if you desire

  6. Make sure the radio button says “Holidays on a range”

  7. Click on the “To date:” input box and select the last date of the closure

  8. Click on “Save”

  9. Confirm that the closures are now in the list on the right
