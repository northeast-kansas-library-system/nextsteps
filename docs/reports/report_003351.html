Name: GHW - Checkout Counts by Patron

Created on: 2020-08-28 17:31:11

Modified on: 2020-08-28 18:01:18

Cache expiry in seconds: 300

Created by: gwilliams

This file generated: 2023-03-24 16:49:10

Notes:

Item counts by patron where the patron has more than 1 item checked out - includes counts by checkout library

  • Shows items currently checked out
  • At the library you specified
  • grouped by patron barcode number
  • sorted by highest total checkout count to lowest checkout count
  • links

Notes:

Does not include INHOUSE or ILL patron categories

SQL: 3351

 1  Select
 2      Concat('<a href="/cgi-bin/koha/circ/circulation.pl?borrowernumber=', borrowers.borrowernumber, '" target="_blank">', borrowers.cardnumber, '</a>') AS LINK,
 3      borrowers.branchcode AS PATRON_HOME_LIBRARY,
 4      borrowers.categorycode AS PATRON_CATEGORYCODE,
 5      Count(Distinct issues.issue_id) As CKO_FROM_ALL_LIBRARIES,
 6      Group_Concat(Distinct bcounts.BCOUNT Order By bcounts.BCOUNT Separator " - ") As CKO_PER_LIBRARY
 7  From
 8      issues Join
 9      (Select
10           issues.borrowernumber,
11           Concat(issues.branchcode, ' - ', Count(issues.itemnumber)) As BCOUNT
12       From
13           issues
14       Group By
15           issues.borrowernumber,
16           issues.branchcode) bcounts On bcounts.borrowernumber = issues.borrowernumber Join
17      borrowers On issues.borrowernumber = borrowers.borrowernumber
18  Where
19      borrowers.categorycode <> 'INHOUSE' And
20      borrowers.categorycode <> 'ILL' And
21      borrowers.branchcode Like <<Choose patron's home library|LBRANCH>>
22  Group By
23      borrowers.cardnumber,
24      borrowers.branchcode,
25      borrowers.categorycode,
26      issues.borrowernumber
27  Having
28      Count(Distinct issues.issue_id) > 1
29  Order By
30      CKO_FROM_ALL_LIBRARIES Desc
