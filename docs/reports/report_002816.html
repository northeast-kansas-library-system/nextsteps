Name: GHW - Quick list of titles by an author

Group: Catalog Records and Items

Sub-group: Shelf Lists

Created on: 2016-11-04 16:40:55

Modified on: 2018-04-16 11:02:00

Created by: gwilliams

This file generated: 2023-03-24 16:49:10

Notes:

What it does generates a list of all titles by an author by brief title

  • Shows items currently in the catalog
  • shows items at a specified library by a specified author
  • grouped by classification, call number, author, and title information
  • sorted by title

Notes:

Report created at the request of Baldwin Public Library.

The author's name must be entered as SURNAME, FIRSTNAME or the report will not work

Click here to run in a new window

SQL: 2816

 1  SELECT
 2    items.location,
 3    items.itype,
 4    authorised_values.lib AS COLLECTION_CODE,
 5    items.itemcallnumber AS CALL_NUMBER,
 6    biblio.author,
 7    biblio.title
 8  FROM
 9    items LEFT JOIN
10    biblioitems
11      ON items.biblioitemnumber = biblioitems.biblioitemnumber LEFT JOIN
12    biblio
13      ON biblioitems.biblionumber = biblio.biblionumber LEFT JOIN
14    authorised_values
15      ON items.ccode = authorised_values.authorised_value
16  WHERE
17    items.homebranch LIKE <<Items owned by|LBRANCH>> AND
18    items.itype LIKE <<Enter item type|LITYPES>> AND
19    biblio.author LIKE concat('%',<<Enter part of author's name in Last, First format>>, '%') AND
20    authorised_values.category = "CCODE"
21  ORDER BY
22    biblio.title
