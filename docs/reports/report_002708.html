Report 002708 contents // Intra-Consortial Loans at a branch during a selected date range

Report technical information: 2708

ID number: 2708

Name: Intra-Consortial Loans at a branch during a selected date range

Group: Circulation

Sub-group: Circ Stats

Created on: 2016-07-12 17:21:02

Modified on: 2016-07-12 18:21:33

Created by: gwilliams

This file generated: 2023-03-24 16:49:10

Notes:

This report counts items that were checked out at a library that did not have a homebranch of that same library.

  • Counts items that were checked out during a specified date range (between 12:00 a.m. on the START DATE and 11:59 p.m. on the END DATE)
  • Counts all items checked out or renewed at a specified branch that were not owned by that branch
  • grouped by check-out-branch, shelving-location, and collection-code
  • sorted by check-out-branch, shelving-location, and collection-code
  • includes rollup totals for each grouping sub-set

Notes:

This report is similar to report 2707 which does the same thing but only shows items checked out or renewed in the previous calendar month.

SQL: 2708

 1  SELECT
 2   CHECK_OUT_BRANCH, SHELVING_LOCATION, COLLECTION_CODE, SUM(C_R) AS CHECKOUTS_PLUS_RENEWALS
 3  FROM
 4   (
 5  SELECT
 6   statistics.branch AS CHECK_OUT_BRANCH, items.location AS SHELVING_LOCATION, statistics.ccode AS COLLECTION_CODE, COUNT(*) AS C_R
 7  FROM
 8   statistics
 9  JOIN items ON statistics.itemnumber = items.itemnumber
10  WHERE
11   (statistics.datetime BETWEEN (@StartDate:= <<between the beginning of the day on "START DATE"|date>>)  AND (@EndDate:= <<and the end of the day on "END DATE"|date>>+ INTERVAL 1 DAY)) AND (statistics.type = 'issue' OR statistics.type = 'renew') AND (items.homebranch <> statistics.branch)
12  GROUP BY CHECK_OUT_BRANCH, SHELVING_LOCATION, COLLECTION_CODE
13
14  UNION
15
16  SELECT
17   statistics.branch AS CHECK_OUT_BRANCH, deleteditems.location AS SHELVING_LOCATION, statistics.ccode AS COLLECTION_CODE, COUNT(*) AS C_R
18  FROM
19   statistics
20  JOIN deleteditems ON statistics.itemnumber = deleteditems.itemnumber
21  WHERE
22   (statistics.datetime BETWEEN @StartDate  AND @EndDate) AND (statistics.type = 'issue' OR statistics.type = 'renew') AND (deleteditems.homebranch <> statistics.branch)
23  GROUP BY CHECK_OUT_BRANCH, SHELVING_LOCATION, COLLECTION_CODE) AS X
24  WHERE CHECK_OUT_BRANCH = <<Select branch|branches>>
25  GROUP BY CHECK_OUT_BRANCH, SHELVING_LOCATION, COLLECTION_CODE WITH ROLLUP
