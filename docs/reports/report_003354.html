Report 003354 contents // GHW - Items marked as “Withdrawn” in the previous calendar month at OTTAWA

Report technical information: 3354

ID number: 3354

Name: GHW - Items marked as “Withdrawn” in the previous calendar month at OTTAWA

Group: OTTAWA

Created on: 2020-09-29 23:37:01

Modified on: 2021-08-16 13:44:19

Cache expiry in seconds: 300

Created by: gwilliams

This file generated: 2023-03-24 16:49:10

Notes:

Generates a list of items that were marked as "Withdrawn" in the previous calendar month at OTTAWA and shows the last patron to have had the item billed to their account

  • Only shows items marked as "Withdrawn" in the previous calendar month
  • where OTTAWA is the item home branch
  • grouped by item number and biblionumber
  • sorted by home library, shelf location, item type, collection code, call number, author, and title
  • contains raw HTML links to the bibliographic record and to the borrower's account page

Notes:

Scheduled to automatically e-mail to OTTAWA staff on the first of each month.

  1  SELECT
  2    Concat('https://staff.nextkansas.org/cgi-bin/koha/catalogue/detail.pl?biblionumber=', items.biblionumber) AS LINK_TO_TITLE,
  3    items.itemnumber AS ITEM_NUMBER,
  4    Concat("-", Coalesce(items.barcode, "-"), "-") AS ITEM_BARCODE,
  5    Concat_Ws(" // ",
  6      items.homebranch,
  7      items.holdingbranch,
  8      Coalesce(locs.lib, "-"),
  9      Coalesce(itypes.description, "-"),
 10      Coalesce(ccodes.lib, "-"),
 11      items.itemcallnumber,
 12      items.copynumber,
 13      biblio.author,
 14      Concat_Ws(" ",
 15        biblio.title,
 16        ExtractValue(biblio_metadata.metadata, '//datafield[@tag="245"]/subfield[@code="h"]'),
 17        ExtractValue(biblio_metadata.metadata, '//datafield[@tag="245"]/subfield[@code="b"]'),
 18        ExtractValue(biblio_metadata.metadata, '//datafield[@tag="245"]/subfield[@code="p"]'),
 19        ExtractValue(biblio_metadata.metadata, '//datafield[@tag="245"]/subfield[@code="n"]')
 20      )
 21    ) AS ITEM_INFO,
 22    items.replacementprice,
 23    Date_Format(fees.date_due, '%Y-%m-%d') AS ITEM_DUE_DATE,
 24    withdrawnst.lib AS WITHDRAWN_STATUS,
 25    items.withdrawn_on,
 26    Concat_Ws(" | ",
 27      Concat("Due on: ", items.onloan),
 28      If(notloanst.lib = "", "", Concat("Not for loan status: ", notloanst.lib)),
 29      Concat("Damaged status: ", damagedst.lib, "on", items.damaged_on),
 30      Concat("Lost status: ", lostst.lib, "on", items.itemlost_on)
 31    ) AS OTHER_STATUSES,
 32    Concat('https://staff.nextkansas.org/cgi-bin/koha/members/pay.pl?borrowernumber=', fees.borrowernumber) AS LINK_TO_BORROWER,
 33    fees.cardnumber,
 34    Concat_Ws(", ", fees.surname, fees.firstname) AS BORROWERNAME,
 35    fees.date AS BILLING_DATE,
 36    fees.amountoutstanding,
 37    fees.branchcode AS CKO_AT,
 38    fees.debit_type_code,
 39    fees.description AS FEE_DESCRIPTION
 40  FROM
 41    items JOIN
 42    biblio ON items.biblionumber = biblio.biblionumber JOIN
 43    biblio_metadata ON items.biblionumber = biblio_metadata.biblionumber JOIN
 44    biblioitems ON items.biblioitemnumber = biblioitems.biblioitemnumber LEFT JOIN
 45    (SELECT
 46        authorised_values.category,
 47        authorised_values.authorised_value,
 48        authorised_values.lib
 49      FROM
 50        authorised_values
 51      WHERE
 52        authorised_values.category = 'NOT_LOAN') notloanst ON
 53        notloanst.authorised_value = items.notforloan LEFT JOIN
 54    (SELECT
 55        authorised_values.category,
 56        authorised_values.authorised_value,
 57        authorised_values.lib
 58      FROM
 59        authorised_values
 60      WHERE
 61        authorised_values.category = 'ccode') ccodes ON ccodes.authorised_value =
 62        items.ccode LEFT JOIN
 63    (SELECT
 64        itemtypes.itemtype,
 65        itemtypes.description
 66      FROM
 67        itemtypes) itypes ON itypes.itemtype = items.itype LEFT JOIN
 68    (SELECT
 69        authorised_values.category,
 70        authorised_values.authorised_value,
 71        authorised_values.lib
 72      FROM
 73        authorised_values
 74      WHERE
 75        authorised_values.category = 'DAMAGED') damagedst ON
 76        damagedst.authorised_value = items.damaged LEFT JOIN
 77    (SELECT
 78        authorised_values.category,
 79        authorised_values.authorised_value,
 80        authorised_values.lib
 81      FROM
 82        authorised_values
 83      WHERE
 84        authorised_values.category = 'LOST') lostst ON lostst.authorised_value =
 85        items.itemlost LEFT JOIN
 86    (SELECT
 87        authorised_values.category,
 88        authorised_values.authorised_value,
 89        authorised_values.lib
 90      FROM
 91        authorised_values
 92      WHERE
 93        authorised_values.category = 'WITHDRAWN') withdrawnst ON
 94        withdrawnst.authorised_value = items.withdrawn LEFT JOIN
 95    (SELECT
 96        authorised_values.category,
 97        authorised_values.authorised_value,
 98        authorised_values.lib
 99      FROM
100        authorised_values
101      WHERE
102        authorised_values.category = 'LOC') locs ON locs.authorised_value =
103        items.location JOIN
104    (SELECT
105        accountlines.itemnumber,
106        accountlines.issue_id,
107        accountlines.borrowernumber,
108        accountlines.date,
109        accountlines.amountoutstanding,
110        old_issues.branchcode,
111        accountlines.debit_type_code,
112        accountlines.description,
113        accountlines.status,
114        borrowers.cardnumber,
115        borrowers.surname,
116        borrowers.firstname,
117        old_issues.date_due
118      FROM
119        accountlines JOIN
120        old_issues ON old_issues.issue_id = accountlines.issue_id JOIN
121        borrowers ON accountlines.borrowernumber = borrowers.borrowernumber
122      WHERE
123        accountlines.debit_type_code = 'LOST') fees ON fees.itemnumber =
124        items.itemnumber
125  WHERE
126    items.homebranch = "OTTAWA" AND
127    withdrawnst.lib <> "" AND
128    Month(items.withdrawn_on) = Month(Now() - INTERVAL 1 MONTH) AND
129    Year(items.withdrawn_on) = Year(Now() - INTERVAL 1 MONTH)
130  GROUP BY
131    items.itemnumber,
132    biblio.biblionumber
133  ORDER BY
134    items.homebranch,

138    items.itemcallnumber,
