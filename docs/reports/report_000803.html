Name: Requested titles sorted from greatest number of requests to lowest number of requests

Group: Popular Reports

Created on: 2010-02-03 11:08:36

Modified on: 2021-04-02 16:08:09

Created by: nekls

Notes:

Generates a list of all bibliographic records with requests sorted by the number of requests on each record

  • Shows records with unfilled reqeusts on them
  • at all Next Search Catalog libraries
  • grouped by biblionumber
  • sorted by number of reqeusts
  • links to the bibliographic records

Notes:

Formerly titled "Most Reserved Titles"

Updated by GHW on 2021.04.02

SQL: 803

 1  SELECT
 2    reservess.Count_reserve_id AS UNFILLED_REQUESTS,
 3    item_counts.Count_itemnumber AS ITEMS_IN_SYSTEM,
 4    biblio.author,
 5    Concat_Ws(" ",
 6      biblio.title,
 7      biblio.subtitle,
 8      biblioitems.number,
 9      biblio.part_name
10    ) AS FULL_TITLE,
11    locs.lib AS BIB_LOCATION,
12    itypess.description AS BIB_ITYPE,
13    ccodes.lib AS CCODE,
14    Date_Format(biblio.datecreated, "%Y.%m.%d") AS DATE_ADDED,
15    item_counts.Group_Concat_replacementprice AS ITEM_COUNT,
16    old_reservess.Count_reserve_id AS PREVIOUSLY_FILLED_REQUESTS,
17    biblio.biblionumber AS BIBLIO_NUMBER,
18    Concat(
19      '<a class= "clicked" href=\"/cgi-bin/koha/catalogue/detail.pl?biblionumber=',
20      biblio.biblionumber,
21      '\" target="_blank">Go to title</a>'
22    ) AS LINK_TO_TITLE
23  FROM
24    biblio JOIN
25    biblioitems ON biblioitems.biblionumber = biblio.biblionumber LEFT JOIN
26    (SELECT
27        authorised_values.category,
28        authorised_values.authorised_value,
29        authorised_values.lib,
30        authorised_values.lib_opac
31      FROM
32        authorised_values
33      WHERE
34        authorised_values.category = 'LOC') locs ON locs.authorised_value =
35        biblioitems.agerestriction LEFT JOIN
36    (SELECT
37        itemtypes.itemtype,
38        itemtypes.description
39      FROM
40        itemtypes) itypess ON itypess.itemtype = biblioitems.itemtype LEFT JOIN
41    (SELECT
42        authorised_values.category,
43        authorised_values.authorised_value,
44        authorised_values.lib,
45        authorised_values.lib_opac
46      FROM
47        authorised_values
48      WHERE
49        authorised_values.category = 'ccode') ccodes ON ccodes.authorised_value =
50        biblioitems.cn_class  JOIN
51    (SELECT
52        items.biblionumber,
53        Count(items.itemnumber) AS Count_itemnumber,
54        GROUP_CONCAT(
55          DISTINCT items.replacementprice
56          ORDER BY items.replacementprice
57          DESC SEPARATOR ' / '
58        ) AS Group_Concat_replacementprice
59      FROM
60        items
61      GROUP BY
62        items.biblionumber) item_counts ON item_counts.biblionumber =
63        biblio.biblionumber  JOIN
64    (SELECT
65        reserves.biblionumber,
66        Count(reserves.reserve_id) AS Count_reserve_id
67      FROM
68        reserves
69      GROUP BY
70        reserves.biblionumber) reservess ON reservess.biblionumber =
71        biblio.biblionumber LEFT JOIN
72    (SELECT
73        old_reserves.biblionumber,
74        Count(old_reserves.reserve_id) AS Count_reserve_id
75      FROM
76        old_reserves
77      WHERE
78        old_reserves.cancellationdate IS NULL
79      GROUP BY
80        old_reserves.biblionumber) old_reservess ON old_reservess.biblionumber =
81        biblio.biblionumber
82  GROUP BY
83    reservess.Count_reserve_id,
84    biblio.biblionumber
85  ORDER BY
86    UNFILLED_REQUESTS DESC
