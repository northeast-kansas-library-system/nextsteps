Report 002911 contents // GHW - Items Waiting Pickup

Report technical information: 2911

ID number: 2911

Name: GHW - Items Waiting Pickup

Group: Holds-Reserves

Created on: 2017-02-23 11:17:34

Modified on: 2018-04-16 11:08:13

Created by: gwilliams

This file generated: 2023-03-24 16:49:10

Notes:

Generates a list of items awaiting pickup

  • Shows items currently on the hold shelf
  • at the pick-up location you specify
  • sorted by date the item was placed on your hold shelf
  • contains links to the requesting patron's account

Click here to run in a new window

SQL: 2911

 1  SELECT
 2    reserves.branchcode AS PICKUP_LOCATION,
 3    reserves.waitingdate AS WAITING_SINCE,
 4    Concat_Ws("<br />", Concat("Title: ", biblio.title), Concat("Author: ", If(biblio.author = "", "-", biblio.author)), Concat_Ws(" | ", "Call#: ", items.location, items.itype, items.ccode, items.itemcallnumber, items.enumchron, items.copynumber), Concat("Barcode: ", UPPER(items.barcode))) AS
 5    ITEM_DETAILS,
 6    Concat_Ws("<br />", Concat(borrowers.surname, ", ", borrowers.firstname), borrowers.cardnumber, borrowers.phone, If(borrowers.email = "", "-", borrowers.email), Concat("<br />", If(contact.attribute IS NULL, "<span style='color:red; font-weight: bold;'>NO CONTACT METHOD SPECIFIED</span>", If(contact.attribute = "Phone", Concat("<span style='color:red; font-weight: bold;'>Please Phone</span>"), If(contact.attribute = "Email", Concat("<span style='color:green; font-weight: bold;'>Patron prefers e-mail</span>"), If(contact.attribute = "Text", Concat("<span style='color:red; font-weight: bold;'>Please Text</span>"), Concat("<span style='color:red; font-weight: bold;'>Prefered contact method: ", "</span>"))))))) AS PATRON_DETAILS,
 7    Concat('<a href=\"/cgi-bin/koha/circ/circulation.pl?borrowernumber=', borrowers.borrowernumber, '\" target="_blank">Open patron account in new window</a>') AS LINK_TO_PATRON
 8  FROM
 9    reserves JOIN
10    items
11      ON reserves.itemnumber = items.itemnumber JOIN
12    biblio
13      ON items.biblionumber = biblio.biblionumber JOIN
14    borrowers
15      ON reserves.borrowernumber = borrowers.borrowernumber LEFT JOIN
16    (SELECT
17      borrower_attributes.borrowernumber,
18      borrower_attributes.code,
19      borrower_attributes.attribute
20    FROM
21      borrower_attributes
22    WHERE
23      borrower_attributes.code = 'HOLD') contact
24      ON borrowers.borrowernumber = contact.borrowernumber
25  WHERE
26    reserves.branchcode LIKE <<Choose branch|LBRANCH>> AND
27    reserves.found = 'W'
28  ORDER BY
29    WAITING_SINCE DESC
