Group: Statistics

Sub-group: Date range

Modified on: 2018-04-16 11:11:31

Cache expiry in seconds: 300

Created by: gwilliams

This file generated: 2023-03-24 16:49:10

Notes:

Counts checkouts + renewals grouped by the patron's birth year

  • Shows data from the date range you specify
  • at the library you specify
  • grouped and sorted by checkout branchcode and borrower's birth year

Notes:

Data from the statistics table is purged after 25 months so this report cannot generate reports for data more than 25 months old. As with all statistical data, the best practice is to run the reports for the data you want within a month or two of the creation of that data and then to save that data as a spreadsheet.

SQL: 2979

 1  SELECT
 2    branches.branchcode,
 3    Coalesce(CIR_RENEW_LM.BIRTHYEAR, "-") AS BORROWERS_BIRTH_YEAR,
 4    Coalesce(CIR_RENEW_LM.count, 0) AS CHECKOUT_RENEW_LM
 5  FROM
 6    branches
 7    LEFT JOIN (SELECT
 8        statistics.branch,
 9        Count(*) AS count,
10        Coalesce(Year(borrowers.dateofbirth), Year(deletedborrowers.dateofbirth)) AS BIRTHYEAR
11      FROM
12        statistics
13        LEFT JOIN borrowers ON statistics.borrowernumber = borrowers.borrowernumber
14        LEFT JOIN deletedborrowers ON statistics.borrowernumber = deletedborrowers.borrowernumber
15      WHERE
16        (statistics.type = 'issue' OR
17          statistics.type = 'renew') AND
18          (statistics.datetime BETWEEN <<Items checked out or renewed between|date>> and (<<and|date>> + interval 1 day))
19      GROUP BY
20        statistics.branch,
21        Coalesce(Year(borrowers.dateofbirth), Year(deletedborrowers.dateofbirth))) CIR_RENEW_LM ON branches.branchcode = CIR_RENEW_LM.branch
22  WHERE
23    branches.branchcode LIKE <<Choose check-out branch|LBRANCH>> AND
24    Coalesce(CIR_RENEW_LM.BIRTHYEAR, "-") LIKE CONCAT("%",<<Enter patron's birth year (or enter "%" for all patrons)>>,"%")
25  GROUP BY
26    branches.branchcode,
27    Coalesce(CIR_RENEW_LM.BIRTHYEAR, "-")
28  ORDER BY
29    branches.branchcode,
30    BORROWERS_BIRTH_YEAR
