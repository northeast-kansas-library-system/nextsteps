Report 002609 contents // Orphaned requests

Report technical information: 2609

ID number: 2609

Name: Orphaned requests

Group: Holds-Reserves

Created on: 2015-09-24 17:47:27

Modified on: 2022-09-29 09:30:01

Created by: hbraum

This file generated: 2023-03-24 16:49:10

Notes:

The report returns patron's holds on records where, at the time of the hold placing, the record had items available to fulfill holds, but for a number of reasons, no items remain available at this time to fulfill the holds, due to:

1) Last item available is overdue by 30+ days.
2) Last item available is marked lost/damaged/withdrawn.
3) Last item available is now not for loan (report ignores items with on-order status)

The report returns a link to your patron's account, the patron's name, when the hold was placed and a link to the record.

Please do the following to clean these up:

1) Determine if your patron still wants the requested material.
2) If the patron does still want the item, delete the hold, and either see if another record of the same title, similar format, is available in NExpress or request through ILL.
3) If your patron does not want the item, please delete the hold.

credit: Jesse Weaver @ ByWater

SQL: 2609

 1  SELECT
 2    Concat(
 3      '<a href="/cgi-bin/koha/members/moremember.pl?borrowernumber=',
 4      reserves.borrowernumber,
 5      '" target="_blank">Link to borrower</a>'
 6    ) AS LINK_TO_BORROWER,
 7    reserves.biblionumber,
 8    Date_Format(reserves.reservedate, "%Y-%m-%d") AS reservedate,
 9    Date_Format(reserves.expirationdate, "%Y-%m-%d") AS expirationdate,
10    Date_Format(reserves.reservedate + INTERVAL 1 YEAR, "%Y-%m-%d") AS one_year,
11    Concat(
12      '<a href="/cgi-bin/koha/reserve/request.pl?biblionumbers=',
13      reserves.biblionumber,
14      '" target="_blank">Link to request</a>'
15    ) AS LINK_TO_BIBLIO
16  FROM
17    (SELECT
18        items.biblionumber,
19        Count(DISTINCT items.itemnumber) AS Count_itemnumber,
20        items.notforloan,
21        items.damaged,
22        items.itemlost,
23        items.withdrawn
24      FROM
25        items
26      WHERE
27        (items.notforloan < 1 OR
28          items.notforloan IS NULL) AND
29        (items.damaged = 0 OR
30          items.damaged IS NULL) AND
31        (items.itemlost = 0 OR
32          items.itemlost IS NULL) AND
33        (items.withdrawn = 0 OR
34          items.withdrawn IS NULL)
35      GROUP BY
36        items.biblionumber,
37        items.notforloan,
38        items.damaged,
39        items.itemlost,
40        items.withdrawn) counts RIGHT JOIN
41    reserves ON counts.biblionumber = reserves.biblionumber
42  WHERE
43    Coalesce(counts.Count_itemnumber, 0) = 0 AND
44    reserves.branchcode LIKE <<Choose your library|LBRANCH>>
45  ORDER BY
46    expirationdate,
47    one_year DESC
