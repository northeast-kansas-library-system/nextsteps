Created on: 2018-09-26 11:42:19

Modified on: 2021-10-26 12:23:30

Cache expiry in seconds: 300

Created by: gwilliams

This file generated: 2023-03-24 16:49:10

Notes:

List of transactions at the circulation desk at the library you specify

  • Shows transactions on the date you specify
  • shows transactions that occurred at the library you specify
  • grouped and sorted by library, time the transaction occurred, and the item number

Notes:

This report cannot be used to show any transactions more than 25 months old.

Shelving location information was not stored in the transaction logs until we upgraded to Koha 17.11 on 2018.07.28.

Report changed to show item permanent location in addition to current location at time of circulation.

SQL: 3125

 1  SELECT
 2    statistics.branch AS TRANSACTION_BRANCH,
 3    If(statistics.type = "issue", "checkout", statistics.type) AS STATISTIC_TYPE,
 4    statistics.datetime,
 5    Upper(Coalesce(items.barcode, deleteditems.barcode)) AS ITEM_BC,
 6    permlocs.lib AS PERMANENT_LOCATION,
 7    locations.lib AS SHELVING_LOCATION,
 8    itemtypes.description AS ITYPE,
 9    ccodes.lib AS CCODE,
10    Coalesce(items.itemcallnumber, deleteditems.itemcallnumber) AS CALL_NUMBER,
11    biblio.author,
12    biblio.title AS `TITLE (245$a only)`,
13    If(deleteditems.barcode IS NOT NULL,
14      "Item has been deleted",
15      If(statistics.type = "payment",
16        "No item data for payments",
17        If(statistics.type = "writeoff",
18          "No item data for writeoffs",
19          If(statistics.type = "return",
20            "Shelving loction not recorded for returns",
21            "-"
22          )
23        )
24      )) AS NOTES
25  FROM
26    statistics LEFT JOIN
27    items ON statistics.itemnumber = items.itemnumber LEFT JOIN
28    deleteditems ON statistics.itemnumber = deleteditems.itemnumber LEFT JOIN
29    (SELECT
30        authorised_values.category,
31        authorised_values.authorised_value,
32        authorised_values.lib
33      FROM
34        authorised_values
35      WHERE
36        authorised_values.category = 'loc') locations ON statistics.location =
37        locations.authorised_value LEFT JOIN
38    (SELECT
39        itemtypes.itemtype,
40        itemtypes.description
41      FROM
42        itemtypes) itemtypes ON statistics.itemtype = itemtypes.itemtype LEFT JOIN
43    (SELECT
44        authorised_values.category,
45        authorised_values.authorised_value,
46        authorised_values.lib
47      FROM
48        authorised_values
49      WHERE
50        authorised_values.category = 'ccode') ccodes ON statistics.ccode =
51        ccodes.authorised_value LEFT JOIN
52    biblio ON items.biblionumber = biblio.biblionumber LEFT JOIN
53    (SELECT
54        authorised_values.category,
55        authorised_values.authorised_value,
56        authorised_values.lib,
57        authorised_values.lib_opac
58      FROM
59        authorised_values
60      WHERE
61        authorised_values.category = 'loc') permlocs ON permlocs.authorised_value = items.permanent_location
62  WHERE
63    statistics.branch like <<Choose the circulating library|branches>> AND
64    statistics.datetime LIKE Concat(<<Choose a date|date>>, "%") AND
65    If(
66      statistics.type = "renew",
67      "renew-issue",
68      If(
69        statistics.type = "issue",
70        "issue-renew", statistics.type
71      )
72    ) LIKE <<Choose a transaction type|LSTATTYPE>>
73  GROUP BY
74    statistics.branch,
75    statistics.datetime,
76    permlocs.lib,
77    statistics.itemnumber
78  ORDER BY
79    TRANSACTION_BRANCH,
80    statistics.datetime,
81    statistics.itemnumber
