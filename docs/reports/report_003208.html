Name: GHW - Policy map report

Created on: 2019-05-29 16:21:54

Modified on: 2019-09-24 14:21:50

Cache expiry in seconds: 300

Created by: gwilliams

This file generated: 2023-03-24 16:49:10

Notes:

Creates borrower address report for use in Policy Map

  • Shows current patron addresses
  • at the library you select
  • grouped by borrowernumber
  • sorted by address

Notes:

  • Patrons with an un-mappable address (PO Box, no address, no city, no state) will not appear in the results
  • Patron data may not map correctly if parts of the address are spelled incorrectly
  • YEAR_LASTSEEN is the year from the last_updated field in the borrowers table - this data has only been available since June of 2019, so it will not start being reliable or valuable until June of 2020
  • YEAR_LASTSEEN is updated whenever the patron account is used for checking out materials, renewing materials, or logging into a SIP enabled system (such as Hoopla, PC Reservation, Libki, or Cybrarian - to name a few)
  • STATUS = Active means that the patron has checked out or renewed materials within the previous 12 months
  • STATUS = Inactive means that the patron has not checked out or renewed any materials in the previous 12 months

SQL: 3208

 1  SELECT
 2    Trim(If(Coalesce(borrowers.address, borrowers.address2) LIKE "PO%", borrowers.address2, borrowers.address)) AS address,
 3    Trim(If(Coalesce(borrowers.address) LIKE "PO%", borrowers.address, borrowers.address2)) AS address2,
 4    borrowers.city,
 5    borrowers.state,
 6    If(Trim(borrowers.zipcode) LIKE "_____-____", Trim(borrowers.zipcode), (If(Trim(borrowers.zipcode) LIKE "_____", Trim(borrowers.zipcode), ""))) AS zipcode,
 7    Year(borrowers.dateenrolled) AS YEAR_ENROLLED,
 8    Year(borrowers.dateexpiry) AS YEAR_EXPIRED,
 9    If(Year(borrowers.lastseen) = 0, "", Year(borrowers.lastseen)) AS YEAR_LASTSEEN,
10    borrowers.sex,
11    borrowers.categorycode,
12    Floor((DateDiff(CurDate(), borrowers.dateofbirth) / 365.25)) AS AGE,
13    borrowers.branchcode AS HOMEBRANCH,
14    If(Floor((DateDiff(CurDate(), borrowers.dateofbirth) / 365.25)) > 17, "Adult", "Minor") AS AGE_GROUP,
15    If(usecount.Count_datetime > 0, "Active", "Inactive") AS STATUS
16  FROM
17    borrowers
18    LEFT JOIN (
19      SELECT
20        statistics.borrowernumber,
21        Count(statistics.datetime) AS Count_datetime
22      FROM
23        statistics
24      WHERE
25        (statistics.type = 'issue' OR
26          statistics.type = 'renew') AND
27        statistics.datetime BETWEEN CurDate() - INTERVAL 1 YEAR AND CurDate() + INTERVAL 1 DAY
28      GROUP BY
29        statistics.borrowernumber
30    ) usecount
31      ON usecount.borrowernumber = borrowers.borrowernumber
32  WHERE
33    borrowers.categorycode <> 'ILL' AND
34    borrowers.categorycode <> 'STAFF' AND
35    borrowers.categorycode <> 'INHOUSE' AND
36    borrowers.branchcode LIKE <<Choose your library|ZBRAN>> AND
37    If(Trim(If(Coalesce(borrowers.address, borrowers.address2) LIKE "PO%", borrowers.address2, borrowers.address)) = "", "X", (If(borrowers.city = "", "Y", (If(borrowers.state = "", "Z", "A"))))) = 'A'
38  GROUP BY
39    If(Floor((DateDiff(CurDate(), borrowers.dateofbirth) / 365.25)) > 17, "Adult", "Minor"),
40    If(usecount.Count_datetime > 0, "Active", "Inactive"),
41    borrowers.borrowernumber
42  ORDER BY
43    If(Trim(If(Coalesce(borrowers.address, borrowers.address2) LIKE "PO%", borrowers.address2, borrowers.address)) = "", "X", (If(borrowers.city = "", "Y", (If(borrowers.state = "", "Z", "A"))))),
44    borrowers.state,
45    borrowers.city,
46    address,
47    address2,
48    zipcode,
49    HOMEBRANCH,
50    borrowers.categorycode
