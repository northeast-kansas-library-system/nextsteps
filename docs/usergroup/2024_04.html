  1. Borrower categories

  • Borrowers in 3 “exempt” categories will be moved to non-exempt categories by May 31

    • “Staff - library associate (exempt)” borrowers will become “Adult - library associate”

    • “Student (exempt)” borrowers at public libraries will become “Student (public library)”

    • “Teacher/Faculty/Administrator (exempt)” borrowers will become “Teacher/Faculty/Administrator (public library)”

  • After this process is complete, “Staff - library associate (exempt)” will be removed from the system

  • After this process is complete, “Student (exempt)” and “Teacher/Faculty/Administrator (exempt)” will be limited to school district borrowers

  • All libraries using “Other” borrower categories will be asked to evaluate their use of those categories to see if they are, in fact, necessary or useful
