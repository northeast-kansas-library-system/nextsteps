Calendar - close a range of dates
=================================

#. On the home page click on "Tools"

    |find_tools|

#. In the right hand column, click on "Calendar"

    |find_calendar|

#. To add a closure on a range of dates, use the calendar to find the starting date for the closure and then click on it

    |date_range_0010|

#. Give the closure a title

    |date_range_0020|

#. Add a description if you desire

    |date_range_0030|

#. Make sure the radio button says "Holidays on a range"

    |date_range_0040|

#. Click on the "To date:" input box and select the last date of the closure

    |date_range_0050|

#. Click on "Save"

    |date_range_0060|

#. Confirm that the closures are now in the list on the right

    |date_range_0070|
