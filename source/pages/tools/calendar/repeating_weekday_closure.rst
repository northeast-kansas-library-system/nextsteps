Close on the same day every week
================================

#. On the home page click on "Tools"
#. In the right hand column, click on "Calendar"
#. To add a closure on every Saturday, click on *any* Saturday on the calendar
#. Give the closure a title
#. Add a description if you desire
#. Make sure the radio button says "Holiday repeated every same day of the week"
#. Click on "Save"
#. Confirm that the closures are now in the list on the right
