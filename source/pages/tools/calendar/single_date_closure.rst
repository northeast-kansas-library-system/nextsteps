Closed on a specific date
=========================

#. On the home page click on "Tools"

    |find_tools|

#. In the right hand column, click on "Calendar"

    |find_calendar|

#. To add a closure on a single date, use the calendar to find the date and then click on the date of the closure

    |single_date_0010.png|

#. Give the closure a title

    |single_date_0020.png|

#. Add a description if you desire

    |single_date_0030.png|

#. Make sure the radio button says "Holiday only on this day"

    |single_date_0040.png|

#. Click on "Save"

    |single_date_0050.png|

#. Confirm that the closure is now in the list on the right

    |single_date_0060.png|
