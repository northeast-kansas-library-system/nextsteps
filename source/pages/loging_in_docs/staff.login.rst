Log in to the staff interface
-----------------------------

#. Open staff.nextkansas.org

  |staff.login.0010.png|

2. Enter your username and password where indicated and click on "Log in"

  |staff.login.0020.png|

3. If you're successful you'll be taken to the home page in the staff interface

  |staff.login.0030.png|

4. If you enter an incorrect login/password combination 5 or more times in a row you will see a "You must reset your password" message on the login page

  - If you are locked out, there will be a link to a video tutorial on resetting your password at the top of the page

  |staff.login.0040.png|
