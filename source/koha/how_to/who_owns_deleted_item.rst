Who owns this deleted item?
===========================

From time-to-time libraries may find items that have a Next Search Catalog barcode number but the item is no longer in the catalog.  There are several reasons that this may happen.  If a borrower keeps a book overdue longer than 45 days and never returns the item, the item will automatically be marked with a lost status.  `LOST STATUS <//glossary.html>`__
