How to log in to Aspen Discovery for your library
=================================================

#. Go to http://nextkansas.org/MyAccount/SelectInterface
#. Choose your library from the list, then click on "Select Library Catalog"
#. Click on "Sign in"
#. Enter your username where it says "Library Card Number"
#. Enter your password where it says "PIN or Password"
#. You have successfully logged in
